What is USF Muma College of Business?

As said in the words of USF itself, “We are transforming minds and transforming students into future alumni who will lead businesses, spearhead initiatives, and be a powerful force in the community.” The University of South Florida’s Muma College of Business prides itself on creating tomorrow’s business thinkers and giving students the skills necessary to become leaders in both business and society. Challenging curriculum, knowledgeable professors with real-world experience, and a sense of community with students of similar interests await students who attend.

AACSB Accredited

AACSB provides international recognized and specialized accreditation for business and accounting programs at all levels of college education. The AACSB Accreditation Standards challenge post-secondary educators to pursue excellence and continous improvement. AACSB Accreditation is known worldwide as the longest standing, most recognized form of specalized and professional accreditation that an instuition can earn. AACSB-accredited schools have opportunities that you simply can't find at other schools.

Global Footprint

USF offers some programs exclusivly for business majors where people can earn business credit hours that apply toward their majors.

Semester Study Abroad Programs

Full semester exchange programs allow students to fully immerse themselves in the culture of their host nation and attend classes at partner institutions. These trips really help the student to understand people and culture and allow them to experience daily life in another country.

Summer Study Abroad Programs

Summer study abroad programs provide all the benefits of the Semester Study Abroad Programs during the summer. The Summer study abroad programs are led by Muma Collee of Business faculty and provide a easy and fun way for students to earn USF credit hours that apply towards business degree programs and USF summer academic requirements.

Internships

Several international internships are avaliable to students. These programs are sponsored by large external organizations. As a resource to help students, several of the more popular internships are listed, but students are responsible for exploring and handling arrangements for these programs.

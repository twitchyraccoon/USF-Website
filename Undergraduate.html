Undergraduate Information

Students pursuing an undergraduate degree at USF have many opportunites and advantages open to them. The college's central location allows for internships, jobs, and education experiences that align with students' specific goals. Those who go into business have to complete an additional 36 credit hour business core that aids them in learning and understanding management responsibilities.

Majors

The USF Muma College of Business, depending on the program, requires around 18-30 credit hours of specialized study for major course work. The college offers 9 undergraduate majors:

  • Accounting
  • Advertising
  • Business Analytics and Information Systems
  • Finance
  • Global Business
  • Management
  • Marketing
  • Personal Financing Planning
  • Pre-med Dual Degree with Biomedical Sciences
